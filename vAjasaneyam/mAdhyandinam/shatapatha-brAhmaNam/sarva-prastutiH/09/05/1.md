## ०१
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

अथा᳘तः पयोव्रत᳘तायै॥  
प᳘योव्रतो दीक्षितः᳘ स्याद्देवे᳘भ्यो ह वा᳘ ऽअमृ᳘तम᳘पचक्राम॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

अथा᳘तः पयोव्रत᳘तायै॥  
प᳘योव्रतो दीक्षितः᳘ स्याद्देवे᳘भ्यो ह वा᳘ अमृ᳘तम᳘पचक्राम॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

1. Now, then, as to the taking of milk as fast-food: the initiated should take milk for his fast-food. Once upon a time the nectar of immortality departed from the gods.
</details>

## ०२
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

ते᳘ होचुः॥  
श्र᳘मेण त᳘पसेदम᳘न्विच्छामे᳘ति तच्छ्र᳘मेण त᳘पसा᳘ ऽन्वैच्छंस्ते᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रता ऽअभवन्नेतद्वै त᳘पो यो᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो᳘ ऽसत्त᳘स्य घो᳘षमा᳘शुश्रुवुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

ते᳘ होचुः॥  
श्र᳘मेण त᳘पसेदम᳘न्विछामे᳘ति तछ्र᳘मेण त᳘पसान्वैछंस्ते᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रता अभवन्नेतद्वै त᳘पो यो᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो᳘ ऽसत्त᳘स्य घो᳘षमा᳘शुश्रुवुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

2. They said, 'Let us seek for it here by toil and penance!' They sought for it by toil and penance. Having become initiated, they were living on fast-milk, for penance it is when, after becoming initiated, one lives on fast-milk. They heard the sound of it.
</details>

## ०३
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(स्ते᳘) ते᳘ होचुः॥  
(र्ने᳘) ने᳘दीयो वै᳘ भवति भू᳘यस्त᳘प ऽउपायामे᳘ति ते त्रीन्त्स्त᳘नानु᳘पेयुस्तत्प᳘राददृशुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

ते᳘ होचुः॥  
ने᳘दीयो वै᳘ भवति भू᳘यस्त᳘प उपायामे᳘ति ते त्रीन्त्स्त᳘नानु᳘पेयुस्तत्प᳘राददृशुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

3. They said, 'It is indeed coming nearer: let us practise penance still further!' They resorted to three teats: they saw it.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

</details>

## ०५
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(स्ते᳘) ते᳘ होचुः॥  
(र्ने᳘) ने᳘दीयो वै᳘ भवति भू᳘यस्त᳘प ऽउ᳘पायामे᳘ति त ऽए᳘कᳫँ᳭ स्त᳘नमु᳘पेयुस्तद᳘धिजगाम न᳘ त्वभि᳘पत्तुᳫँ᳭[[!!]] शेकुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

ते᳘ होचुः॥  
ने᳘दीयो वै᳘ भवति भू᳘यस्त᳘प उपायामे᳘ति त ए᳘कᳫं स्त᳘नमु᳘पेयुस्तद᳘धिजगाम न त्व᳘भि᳘पत्तुं शेकुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

5. They said, 'It is indeed coming nearer: let us practise penance still further!' They resorted to one teat: it came nigh unto them, but they could not lay hold of it.
</details>

## ०६
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(स्ते᳘) ते᳘ होचुः॥ 
(र᳘) अ᳘धि वा᳘ ऽअगन्न᳘ त्वभि᳘पत्तुᳫँ᳭[[!!]] शक्नुमः स᳘र्व्वं त᳘प ऽउ᳘पायामे᳘ति त᳘ ऽउपवसथे᳘ ऽनाशकमु᳘पेयुरेतद्वै स᳘र्व्वं त᳘पो यद᳘नाशकस्त᳘स्मादुपवसथे᳘ नाश्नीयात्॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

ते᳘ होचुः अ᳘धि वा᳘ अगन्न त्व᳘भि᳘पत्तुं शक्नुमः स᳘र्वं त᳘प उपायामे᳘ति त᳘ उपवसथे᳘ ऽनाशकमु᳘पेयुरेतद्वै स᳘र्वं त᳘पो यद᳘नाशकस्त᳘स्मादुपवसथेॗ नाश्नीयात्॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

6. They said, 'It has indeed come nigh unto us, but we cannot lay hold of it: let us undergo the whole (practice of) penance!' On the day of preparation they underwent entire abstention from food; for the whole (practice of) penance it is when one abstains from food: let him therefore eat nothing on the day of preparation.
</details>

## ०७
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(त्त᳘) त᳘त्प्रात᳘रभिप᳘द्य॥  
(द्या) अभिषु᳘त्याग्ना᳘वजुहवुस्त᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तमदधुः स᳘र्व्वेषामु हैष᳘ देवा᳘नामात्मा य᳘दग्निस्तद्य᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तम᳘दधुस्त᳘दात्म᳘न्नमृ᳘तमदधत त᳘तो देवा᳘ ऽअमृ᳘ता ऽअभवन्॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

त᳘त्प्रात᳘रभिप᳘द्य॥  
अभिषु᳘त्याग्ना᳘वजुहवुस्त᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तमदधुः स᳘र्वेषामु हैष᳘ देवा᳘नामात्मा य᳘दग्निस्तद्य᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तम᳘दधुस्त᳘दात्म᳘न्नमृ᳘तमदधत त᳘तो देवा᳘ अमृ᳘ता अभवन्॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

7. On the morrow, having laid hold of it and pressed it (the Soma), they offered (of it) in the fire,

and thereby bestowed immortality upon Agni. And he, Agni (the fire-altar), indeed, is the body of all the gods; and hence, when they bestowed immortality on Agni (the fire-altar), they bestowed immortality on their own selves, and thereby the gods became immortal.
</details>

## ०८
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(वंस्त) तद्यत्त᳘दमृ᳘तᳫँ᳭ सो᳘मः सः॥  
(०) त᳘दद्या᳘पि य᳘जमानः श्र᳘मेण त᳘पसा᳘ ऽन्विच्छति स᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो भवत्येत᳘द्वै त᳘पो यो᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो᳘ ऽसत्त᳘स्य घो᳘षमा᳘शृणोतीत्यहे᳘ क्रय ऽइ᳘ति॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

तद्यत्त᳘दमृ᳘तᳫं सो᳘मः सः॥  
त᳘दद्या᳘पि य᳘जमानः श्र᳘मेण त᳘पसा᳘न्विछति स᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो भवत्येतद्वै त᳘पो यो᳘ दीक्षित्वा प᳘योव्रतो᳘ ऽसत्त᳘स्य घो᳘षमा᳘शृणोतीत्यहे᳘ क्रय इ᳘ति॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

8. Now that same nectar of immortality is Soma. And even to this day the Sacrificer seeks for it by toil and penance; having become initiated he lives on fast-milk; for penance it is when, after being initiated, one lives on fast-milk; he hears the sound of it, saying, 'On such and such a day the buying (will take place [^egg_448])!'

[^egg_448]: 256:1 That is, the buying of the Soma plants, for which see part ii, p. 69 seq.
</details>

## ०९
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

स त्रीन्त्स्त᳘नानु᳘पैति॥  
तत्प᳘रापश्यति स द्वौ स्त᳘ना ऽउ᳘पैति तन्ने᳘दीयसः प᳘रापश्यति स ऽए᳘कᳫँ᳭ स्त᳘नमु᳘पैति तद᳘धिगच्छति न᳘ त्वभि᳘पत्तुᳫँ᳭[[!!]] शक्नोति स᳘ ऽउपवसथे᳘ ऽनाशकमु᳘पैत्येतद्वै स᳘र्व्वं त᳘पो यद᳘नाशकस्त᳘स्मादुपवसथे᳘ नाश्नीयात्॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

स त्रीन्त्स्त᳘नानु᳘पैति॥  
तत्प᳘रापश्यति स द्वौ स्त᳘ना उ᳘पैति तन्ने᳘दीयसः प᳘रापश्यति स ए᳘कᳫं स्त᳘नमु᳘पैति तद᳘धिगछति न त्व᳘भि᳘पत्तुं शक्नोति स᳘ उपवसथे᳘ ऽनाशकमु᳘पैत्येतद्वै स᳘र्वं त᳘पो यद᳘नाशकस्त᳘स्मादुपवसथेॗ नाश्नीयात्॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

9. He resorts to three teats (of the cow): he sees it. He resorts to two teats: he sees it nearer by. He resorts to one teat: it comes nigh to him, but he cannot lay hold of it. He undergoes entire abstinence from food; for the whole (practice of) penance it is when one abstains from food: let him therefore eat nothing on the day of preparation.
</details>

## १०
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(त्त᳘) त᳘त्प्रात᳘रभिप᳘द्य॥  
(द्या) अभिषु᳘त्याग्नौ᳘ जुहोति त᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तं दधात्य᳘थ भक्षयति त᳘दात्म᳘न्नमृ᳘तं धत्ते᳘ सो ऽमृ᳘तो भवत्येतद्वै᳘ मनु᳘ष्यस्यामृ᳘तत्वं[[!!]] यत्स᳘र्व्वमायुरे᳘ति[[!!]] त᳘थो हाने᳘नात्म᳘ना स᳘र्व्वमा᳘युरेति॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

त᳘त्प्रात᳘रभिप᳘द्य॥  
अभिषु᳘त्याग्नौ᳘ जुहोति त᳘दग्ना᳘वमृ᳘तं दधात्य᳘थ भक्षयति त᳘दात्म᳘न्नमृ᳘तं धत्तेॗ सो ऽमृ᳘तो भवत्येतद्वै᳘ मनुष्य᳘स्यामृ᳘तत्वं यत्स᳘र्वमा᳘युरे᳘ति त᳘थो हाने᳘नात्म᳘ना स᳘र्वमा᳘युरेति॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

10. And, on the morrow, having laid hold of it, and pressed it, he offers (of it) in the fire, and thereby bestows immortality on Agni. He then drinks (of it), and thereby bestows immortality on his own self, and becomes immortal; for this, assuredly, is immortality to man when he attains the whole (perfect) life: and so, in truth, he attains the whole life by this self of his.
</details>

## ११
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(त्य) अग्नौ᳘ हुत्वा᳘ ऽथ भक्षयति॥  
दै᳘वो वा᳘ ऽअस्यैष᳘ ऽआत्मा᳘ मानु᳘षो ऽयं᳘ देवा᳘ ऽउ वा ऽअग्रे᳘ ऽथ मनु᳘ष्यास्त᳘स्मादग्नौ᳘ हुत्वा᳘ ऽथ भक्षयति॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

अग्नौ᳘ हुत्वा᳘थ भक्षयति॥  
दै᳘वो वा᳘ अस्यैष᳘ आत्मा᳘ मानुॗषो ऽयं᳘ देवा᳘ उ वा अग्रे᳘ ऽथ मनुॗष्यास्त᳘स्मादग्नौ᳘ हुत्वा᳘थ भक्षयति॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

11. When he has offered in the fire, he drinks (Soma); for that (fire-altar) is his divine body, and this (Sacrificer's own body) is his human one; and

the gods were first, and then men: therefore he drinks (Soma), after offering in the fire.
</details>

## १२
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

(त्य) अथा᳘तः समिष्टयजु᳘षामेव᳘ मीमाᳫँ᳭सा᳘॥  
देवाश्चा᳘सुराश्चोभ᳘ये प्राजापत्याः᳘ प्रजा᳘पतेः पितु᳘र्दायमु᳘पेयुर्व्वा᳘चमेव᳘ सत्यानृते᳘ सत्यं᳘ चैवा᳘नृतं च त᳘ ऽउभ᳘य ऽएव᳘ सत्यम᳘वदन्नुभये᳘ ऽनृतं ते᳘ ह सदृ᳘शं व᳘दन्तः सदृ᳘शा ऽए᳘वासुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

अथा᳘तः समिष्टयजु᳘षामेव᳘ मीमांसा᳟॥  
देवाश्चा᳘सुराश्चोभ᳘ये प्राजापत्याः᳘ प्रजा᳘पतेः पितु᳘र्दायमु᳘पेयुर्वा᳘चमेव᳘ सत्यानृते᳘ सत्यं᳘ चैवा᳘नृतं च त᳘ उभ᳘य एव᳘ सत्यम᳘वदन्नुभये᳘ ऽनृतं ते᳘ ह सदृ᳘शं व᳘दन्तः सदृ᳘शा एॗवासुः॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

12. Now, then, the discussion of the Samishṭayajus (oblations): The gods and the Asuras, both of them sprung from Prajāpati, entered upon their father Prajāpati's inheritance, to wit, speech--truth and untruth, both truth and untruth: they, both of them, spake the truth, and they both spake untruth; and, indeed, speaking alike, they were alike.
</details>

## १३

(ᳫँ᳭) सत्य᳘मन्वा᳘लेभिरे᳘ ऽसुरा ऽउ होत्सृ᳘ज्य स᳘त्यम᳘नृतमन्वा᳘लेभिरे॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

सत्य᳘मन्वा᳘लेभिरे᳘ ऽसुरा उ होत्सृ᳘ज्य सत्यम᳘नृतमन्वा᳘लेभिरे॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

13. The gods relinquished untruth, and held fast to truth, and the Asuras relinquished truth, and held fast to untruth.
</details>

## १४
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

त᳘द्धेद᳘ᳫँ᳘ सत्य᳘मीक्षां᳘चक्रे॥  
यद᳘सुरेष्वा᳘स देवा वा᳘ ऽउत्सृज्या᳘नृतᳫँ᳭ सत्य᳘मन्वा᳘लप्सत ह᳘न्त तद᳘यानी᳘ति त᳘द्देवाना᳘जगाम᳘॥
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

त᳘द्धेद᳘ᳫं᳘ सत्य᳘मीक्षां᳘ चक्रे॥  
यद᳘सुरेष्वा᳘स देवा वा᳘ उत्सृज्या᳘नृतᳫं सत्य᳘मन्वा᳘लप्सत ह᳘न्त तद᳘यानी᳘ति त᳘द्देवाना᳘जगाम॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

14. The truth which was in the Asuras beheld this, and said, 'Verily, the gods have relinquished untruth, and held fast to truth: well, then, I will go thither!' Thus it went over to the gods.
</details>

19. They prepared the Initiation-offering. But the Asuras became aware of it, and said, 'Having made up the sacrifice, the gods are now spreading out that truth: come, let us fetch hither what was ours!' The Samishṭayajus of that (offering) was not yet performed, when they arrived: whence people offer no Samishṭayajus [^egg_452] for that sacrifice. The gods, espying the Asuras, snatched up the sacrifice, and began doing something else [^egg_453]. They (the Asuras) went away again, thinking, 'It is something else they are doing.'

[^egg_452]: 258:3 See III, 1, 3, 6, where the injunction is given that no Samishṭayajus should be performed for the Dīkshaṇīyeshṭi, 'lest he who has put on the garment of initiation should reach the end of the sacrifice before its completion; for the Samishṭayajus is the end of the sacrifice.' It should be remembered that the initiation-offering, however essential, is merely a preliminary ceremony of the Soma-sacrifice, at the end of which latter sacrifice nine Samishṭayajus oblations are offered (IV, 4, 4, 1 seq.) instead of the single one offered at the ordinary haviryajña. The term signifies 'the formula (yajus) of the completed offering (samishṭa).'

[^egg_453]: 258:4 Prof. Delbrück, Altind. Syntax, p. 429, makes this last clause part of the Asuras’ speech or  thoughts,--'one thing they have undertaken to do, and another they are doing.' This can hardly be right.

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

63. 'A six-month (Agni) is the last [^egg_502] he may build,' they say, 'for six-month embryos are the last [^egg_502] that live when born.' If he were to recite the Great Litany on one not carried for a year, he should recite (only) the eighties of verses, for something incomplete is (the Agni) not carried for a year, and something incomplete are the eighties of verses [^egg_503]. But, indeed, he would only still further pull asunder that (Agni, already) pulled asunder [^egg_504]; and, indeed, whether he (Agni) be carried for a year, or not carried for a year, he (the Hotr̥) should recite the whole of the Great Litany.

[^egg_502]: 273:1 That is to say, he must have been carried about for at least six months; and embryos less than six months old cannot live.

[^egg_503]: 273:2 This is so for the reason that the Mahad uktham consists of more than the eighties of verses; see IX, 3, 3, 19. One might feel inclined to include this whole sentence in the preceding quotation.

[^egg_504]: 273:3 That is, already too much attenuated, by being made as large as one a year old (?).
</details>

## ६८
<details><summary>मूलम् - श्रीधरादि</summary>

का᳘मम्वे᳘वैनᳫँ᳭ स चिन्वीत॥  
यः᳘ संव्वत्सरं᳘ जातः स्या᳘त्प्राणो वा᳘ ऽअग्निस्त᳘मेत᳘द्बिभर्त्य᳘थ ह वै रे᳘तः सिक्तं᳘ प्रा᳘णो ऽन्व᳘वरोहति त᳘द्विन्दते तद्य᳘ज्जातं᳘ जातं व्विन्द᳘ते त᳘स्माज्जात᳘वेदास्त᳘स्माद᳘प्येवंवित्का᳘मᳫँ᳭ सद्योभृ᳘तं चिन्वीत य᳘दु ह वा᳘ ऽएवंवित्पि᳘बति वा पाय᳘यति वा त᳘दे᳘वास्य य᳘था स᳘र्वस्मिञ्छाण्डि᳘ले ऽग्नौ सं᳘चिते पक्षपुच्छ᳘वत्या᳘हुतयो हुताः स्यु᳘रेव᳘मस्यैता ऽआ᳘हुतयो हुता᳘ भवन्ति॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

68. And, indeed, let him by all means build who was born a year (after conception); for Agni is the breath; it is thus him (Agni) he holds. And, indeed, as the breath, he enters into the infused seed, and takes possession of it; and inasmuch as he takes possession (vid) of every one that is born (jāta), he is Jātavedas. Wherefore by all means let even one who knows this build him as one ever carried (within him). And, indeed, if one who knows this either drinks (Soma), or offers drink to any one else, these libations of his will be offered even as would be his libations, when offered on a complete Śāṇḍila fire-altar built up with wings and tail.
</details>
